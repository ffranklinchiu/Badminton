Evolution of Playing Styles

1950s-1970s

Classic style with emphasis on clears and drops, using wooden rackets with smaller sweet spots. Players relied more on accuracy than power.

1980s-1990s

Introduction of metal and composite rackets enabled more aggressive play. The Chinese developed fast, attacking styles while Europeans focused on tactical play.

2000s-Present

Modern power game with lightning-fast exchanges. Players combine explosive athleticism with technical precision. The rally point system made matches more intense.
