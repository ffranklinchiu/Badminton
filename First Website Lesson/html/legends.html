The Immortals of Badminton

Players who redefined what was possible on court

Hall of Fame

Pioneers (1950s-1980s)

Rudy Hartono

Indonesia

8-time All England champion (1968-1976), dominated men's singles during the wooden racket era

Liem Swie King

Indonesia

Pioneer of the jump smash, 3-time All England champion, revolutionized attacking play

Golden Age (1990s-2000s)

Susi Susanti

Indonesia

First Olympic gold medalist (1992), dominated women's singles with graceful yet powerful play

Peter Gade

Denmark

World No. 1 for 3 years, brought European flair to the Asian-dominated sport

Zhang Ning

China

2-time Olympic gold medalist (2004, 2008), known for her incredible stamina and mental toughness

Modern Masters (2010s-Present)

Lin Dan

China

Only player to complete "Super Grand Slam", 2 Olympic golds, 5 World Championships

Lee Chong Wei

Malaysia

World No. 1 for 349 weeks, 3 Olympic silver medals, known for his incredible speed

Carolina Marín

Spain

3-time World Champion, Olympic gold medalist, brought European women's badminton to the top
